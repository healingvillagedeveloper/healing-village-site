Mochi Merge

Combine mochis, care for them, and discover soothing little surprises. Simple controls, soft feedback, and a no-pressure flow that feels like a gentle hug for your mind.

  • Platforms: Web; iOS/Android (in preparation)
  • Playtime: from one minute to forever — perfect for breaks
  • Features: offline progress, collection, daily challenges
  • Mood: Calm, meditative, joyful
